About Luxe & Bloom

Where Indian heritage meets global beauty standards, creating a premium salon experience like no other

Our Heritage Story

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Mumbai, Luxe & Bloom was born from a vision to blend traditional Indian beauty secrets with contemporary global trends. Our founder, Priya Sharma, dreamed of creating a sanctuary where clients could experience the richness of India's beauty heritage while enjoying world-class luxury.

Starting as a small studio in Bandra with just three chairs, we've grown into a multi-city premium salon chain with locations in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. Our journey is rooted in the belief that beauty is not just about appearance, but about holistic wellbeing and self-expression.

We proudly incorporate ancient Indian beauty rituals—like Ayurvedic hair treatments, traditional head massage techniques, and natural herbal remedies—into our modern service offerings. This unique fusion has earned us recognition as innovators in the beauty industry.

Today, we serve over 15,000 loyal clients and have trained more than 200 beauty professionals in our signature techniques that honor both tradition and innovation.

Meet Our Founder

Priya Sharma

Founder & Creative Director

Priya Sharma's journey in the beauty industry began at her grandmother's side in Kerala, learning traditional hair care recipes using coconut oil, hibiscus, and amla. After completing her cosmetology education in London and working with top salons in Paris and New York, she returned to India with a mission: to create a luxury salon that honors Indian beauty traditions while meeting global standards.

With over 15 years of international experience, Priya has trained under world-renowned hair artists and brings a unique perspective that combines Eastern wisdom with Western techniques. Her vision for Luxe & Bloom was to create more than a salon—it's a beauty ecosystem where clients feel nurtured, understood, and transformed.

"Beauty is the harmony between tradition and innovation. At Luxe & Bloom, we don't just style hair—we celebrate heritage while embracing the future of beauty."
Priya Sharma
Founder & Creative Director, Luxe & Bloom
