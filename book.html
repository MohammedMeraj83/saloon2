Booking FAQs

Common questions about our Indian salon booking process

How early should I book for Indian bridal services?

For traditional Indian bridal makeup and hair, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during wedding season (October-March). Mehndi appointments should be booked at least 1 week in advance.

What is your payment policy for bridal bookings?

Bridal bookings require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your date. The remaining balance is due on the day of the service. We accept all major payment methods including UPI, cards, and bank transfers.

Can I bring family members for my appointment?

Yes, you can bring 1-2 family members. For bridal appointments, we have a comfortable waiting area. However, for individual services, we recommend limiting companions to ensure a relaxing experience for all clients.

Do you offer home services for bridal appointments?

Yes, we offer home services for bridal makeup and hair with an additional travel charge based on location. Home services must be booked at least 4 weeks in advance and require a 60% deposit.

What are your festival hours and pricing?

During major Indian festivals (Diwali, Eid, Christmas, New Year), we operate with extended hours and have special festival packages. Festival bookings open 1 month in advance and may have premium pricing.

Do you accommodate special needs or allergies?

Absolutely. We use hypoallergenic products and can accommodate special needs. Please mention any allergies, skin conditions, or special requirements in the "Additional Notes" section when booking.
