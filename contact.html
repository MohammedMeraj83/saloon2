Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our Indian salon services

Do I need an appointment for traditional services?

For regular services, appointments are recommended. For Indian bridal makeup, mehndi, and Ayurvedic treatments, appointments are mandatory and should be booked 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during wedding season.

What Indian payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (INR), all major credit/debit cards, UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), NetBanking, and digital wallets. For bridal bookings, we require a 50% deposit via bank transfer or UPI.

Do you offer gift certificates for Indian festivals?

Yes! We offer special gift certificates for Diwali, Eid, Christmas, and wedding seasons. They can be customized with traditional designs and are valid for one year. Available both physical and digital.

Is there parking available at your Mumbai salon?

Yes, we provide complimentary valet parking for all clients. We also have secure underground parking with CCTV surveillance. During peak hours and festivals, we recommend using our valet service.

Can family members accompany for bridal appointments?

Absolutely! We encourage brides to bring 2-3 family members. We have a luxurious waiting area with traditional Indian seating, refreshments, and privacy for pre-wedding rituals and photography.

Do you offer home services in Mumbai?

Yes, we provide premium home services for bridal makeup, mehndi, and hairstyling across Mumbai. Additional charges apply based on location. Home services must be booked at least 2 weeks in advance.
